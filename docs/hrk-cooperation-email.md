## English version (for your own reference — send the German one)

Subject: Hochschulkompass data-cooperation enquiry — study-programme data for international applicants

Dear Sir or Madam,

My name is Nasir; I represent AlmiWorld (almiworld.com), a provider of free
online preparation and orientation services for international students and
skilled workers who wish to come to Germany. Our service AlmiGoethe helps users
understand and prepare for the German-language requirements (Goethe-Zertifikat,
A1–C2) for study and work in Germany.

To give international applicants reliable, up-to-date information on the entry and
language requirements of German degree programmes, we would like to use the data
of the Hochschulkompass. We are aware that commercial or systematic use of the
data requires the explicit consent of the Stiftung zur Förderung der HRK, and we
therefore wish to take the correct route via an official data cooperation — as
already exists with partners such as the Bundesagentur für Arbeit, the DAAD and
ZEIT ONLINE.

Specifically, we would display the programme data (in particular programme,
institution, field of study, language of instruction, and entry/language
requirements) with a clear source attribution "Data: Hochschulkompass / HRK" and
a back-link to hochschulkompass.de. We do not seek exclusive use.

We would be grateful for information on:
1. The licence / terms of use for such a data cooperation and any costs.
2. The available data format / interface (e.g. XML/CSV export or API) and the
   update frequency.
3. Your requirements for attribution and linking.
4. The next steps to conclude a cooperation agreement.

I am happy to provide further materials about AlmiWorld or to speak by phone.
Thank you for your time and support.

Kind regards,
Nasir — AlmiWorld — almiworld.com — [email / phone]
